About Me

Developer, designer, and obsessive builder of interfaces that feel right.

Zhang Wei

I'm a product engineer based in Shanghai, working at the intersection of design and development. For the past six years, I've been building design systems, shipping SaaS products, and occasionally writing about both.

My work is driven by a belief that the best software feels inevitable — as if it couldn't have been designed any other way. I spend a lot of time thinking about design tokens, component APIs, and how to make complex systems feel simple.

Previously, I led frontend architecture at two Series B startups, where I scaled design systems from 0 to 200+ components and 150+ engineers. Before that, I studied Computer Science at Fudan University.

When I'm not at a keyboard, I'm probably cycling, reading about spatial computing, or experimenting with generative art.
